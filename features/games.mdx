## Time Controls

Lichess supports a wide range of time controls to match your playing style:

### Speed Categories

<Accordion title="UltraBullet">
  Lightning-fast games under 30 seconds total. Requires quick reflexes and instant decision-making.
  - **Example**: 15+0 (15 seconds, no increment)
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Bullet">
  Very fast games between 30 seconds and 3 minutes total.
  - **Examples**: 1+0, 2+1
  - Rated games require at least 20 completed rated bullet games to participate
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Blitz">
  Fast-paced games between 3 and 8 minutes.
  - **Examples**: 3+0, 3+2, 5+0, 5+3
  - Most popular time control on Lichess
  - Rated games require at least 15 completed rated blitz games
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Rapid">
  Moderate pace games between 8 and 25 minutes.
  - **Examples**: 10+0, 10+5, 15+10
  - Allows for more calculation and planning
  - Rated games require at least 10 completed rated rapid games
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Classical">
  Long, thoughtful games over 25 minutes.
  - **Examples**: 30+0, 30+20
  - Suitable for serious, in-depth play
  - Rated games require at least 5 completed rated classical games
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Correspondence">
  Games played over days with time measured in days per move.
  - Multiple days to make each move
  - Perfect for analyzing positions deeply
</Accordion>

<Note>
  The minimum rated games requirement ensures fair matchmaking and prevents rating manipulation.
</Note>

## Chess Variants

Beyond standard chess, Lichess supports 10+ chess variants:

- **Standard** - Classic FIDE chess rules
- **Chess960** - Fischer Random with randomized starting positions
- **Crazyhouse** - Captured pieces can be dropped back on the board
- **King of the Hill** - Control the center to win
- **Three-check** - Give check three times to win
- **Antichess** - Lose all your pieces or get stalemated to win
- **Atomic** - Captures trigger explosions
- **Horde** - White has 36 pawns vs Black's normal army
- **Racing Kings** - Race your king to the 8th rank
- **From Position** - Start from custom positions

## Game Features

### During the Game

<Steps>
  <Step title="Move with Drag & Drop">
    Drag pieces to make moves, or click-click for touch devices
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Clock Management">
    Games automatically start the clock when both players have moved. Increment is added after each move.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Premove & Predrop">
    Queue your next move in advance to save time in fast games
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Chat & Emojis">
    Communicate with your opponent (can be disabled in preferences)
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Game Actions

- **Offer Draw** - Propose a draw after move 2 (limited to prevent spam)
- **Request Takeback** - Ask to undo the last move (casual games only)
- **Resign** - Concede the game
- **Abort** - Cancel the game if fewer than 2 moves played
- **Berserk** - In arena tournaments, halve your clock for extra points

<Note>
  Draw offers cannot be repeated too frequently. There's a 20-ply cooldown between offers.
</Note>

### Blindfold Mode

Enable blindfold mode to hide pieces and test your visualization skills. Available as a per-game option.

## Game Analysis

After each game, access powerful analysis tools:

- **Computer Analysis** - Request Stockfish evaluation of your game
- **Move Times** - Review time spent on each move
- **Opening Explorer** - See opening statistics and master games
- **Accuracy** - View your accuracy percentage based on engine evaluations
- **Blur Detection** - System tracks suspicious tabbing behavior

## Finding Games

### Create Custom Game

1. Click "Create a game" from the homepage
2. Select time control and increment
3. Choose rated/casual and variant
4. Set color preference or play random
5. Wait for an opponent or send invite link

### Quick Pairing

Use the lobby to find games instantly:
- Select your preferred time control
- System matches you with a similarly-rated opponent
- Game starts automatically when matched

### Play with Friend

1. Click "Play with a friend"
2. Configure game settings
3. Share the generated URL
4. Game starts when your friend joins

## Game History & Export

All your games are saved permanently:

- Browse game history on your profile
- Filter by result, variant, speed, or opponent
- Export games in PGN format
- Download individual games or bulk export
- Import games to studies for deeper analysis

<Note>
  Games with fewer than 5 moves are not rated to prevent rating manipulation.
</Note>

## Compatibility

### Board Support

Certain time controls are compatible with DGT electronic boards:
- Blitz or slower (3+0 and above)
- Must be rated games against human opponents

### Bot Support

Bots can play in most time controls except ultra-fast games. API access is required for bot accounts.

## Fair Play

Lichess employs multiple systems to ensure fair gameplay:

- **Blur tracking** - Detects when players tab away from the game
- **Move time analysis** - Unusual patterns trigger review
- **Hold alerts** - Long pauses between moves are flagged
- **Engine detection** - Statistical analysis identifies computer assistance

Games older than 21 days with no moves are automatically marked as abandoned.
